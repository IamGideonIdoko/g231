FEATURES

Multi-Platform Compatibility

Whether you game on PC or console, the G231 has you covered. One pair is all you need to get great gaming audio, however you choose to play.

Game-Quality Stereo Sound

With 40 mm neodymium drivers, G230 delivers crisp, clear stereo sound that makes your game come alive.

Comfortable and Breathable

Marathon gaming sessions mean heat and sweat. We covered the G231 ear cups with carefully selected high performance cloth for a comfortable, soft touch even after hours of use. And they slip off easily for washing to keep the headset in top shape.

Lightweight Design

Earpieces have been designed to be slim and light, without skimping on audio quality. A lighter, balanced assembly means you stay focused on the game. You might even forget you’re wearing a gaming headset.

Folding, Noise-Cancelling Mic

Be heard loud and clear without the distraction of background noise. Adjust the mic to pick up only your voice so your teammates can hear you loud and clear.

On-Cable Controls

Keep the mute switch and volume dial close at hand for quick, precise audio adjustments. All attached to a 2-meter cable.

Lay-Flat Earpieces

For a personalized fit, the earpieces swivel up to 90 degrees. And because they can turn to lay flat, they’re less prone to breakage from everyday wear and tear.
